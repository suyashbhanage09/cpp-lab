C++ program demonstrates the use of various relational operators. It compares two integers, a and b, and outputs the results of the comparisons. The operators include equal to, greater than, less than, and their respective variations.

Features
Demonstrates the following relational operators:
Equal to (==)
Greater than (>)
Greater than or equal to (>=)
Less than (<)
Less than or equal to (<=)
Not equal to (!=)
Displays the results of the comparisons clearly.
program initializes two integers, a (6) and b (4), and evaluates various relational expressions. The results are printed to the console, indicating whether each expression is true or false.
